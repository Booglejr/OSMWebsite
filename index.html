Welcome to Order of Space Magic!

Since you're here you are obviously a good programmer.

What we do.

The Order of Space Magic has been collecting some of the finest resources across the interwebs to better benefit the Space Magic community. And you as a member can contribute your google findings here to help other members in their space magical adventures in development.

How can you contribute?

Once you have found an awesome guide or tool you can fork the repo. Now edit _data/entries.yml with the following format:

- name: Cool Guide or Tool Name here tags: ['awesometag1', 'awesometag2'] #These should be named after the tagnames in categories.yml. url: 'https://your.epicresource.urlhere' description: 'A nice little description to tell people what the tool or guide does.' submitter: Your Hancock

You can add new tags but provide a good reason or we won't accept your pull request.

Now commit your change and open a pull request to the project! If everything is in order we will appove it and your tool will be listed here. We look forward to seeing what you have to share!
